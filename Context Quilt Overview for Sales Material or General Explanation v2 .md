Context Quilt Overview: A Cognitive Memory Layer for Your AI EcosystemThe Fundamental Problem: AI Amnesia

**The Challenge:** You've invested heavily in AI (chatbots, internal tools, automation), but does your AI remember your users from one day to the next? For most companies, the answer is no.

* **Every interaction is a "First Date":** Your support bot sees a stranger; your internal knowledge base is new.  
* **Serious Business Consequences:**  
  * **Eroded Trust:** Frustrated customers hate repeating themselves.  
  * **Massive Inefficiency:** Expensive LLMs re-learn basic facts in every conversation, driving up token costs.  
  * **Fragmented Knowledge:** Insights gained by the sales bot are lost to the support bot. There is no unified, 360-degree view of your user.

The Solution: ContextQuilt

ContextQuilt provides a **Cognitive Memory Layer** for your entire AI ecosystem. We are not another LLM; we are the **persistent, long-term memory** that makes all your AIs smarter, more personal, and more efficient.Core Innovation: The Dual-Path Architecture (The Restaurant Analogy)

We use a dual-path architecture to ensure high performance without slowing down your applications.

| Path | Description (Restaurant) | Function (AI System) |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Cold Path** | The complex, time-consuming work (chopping, prepping) done in the afternoon. | **Learns and Organizes:** After an interaction, the application sends the conversation log. The Asynchronous Cognitive Engine works in the background with zero user impact, organizing understanding into three layers. |
| **Hot Path** | The line cook quickly grabs pre-prepped ingredients to assemble the dish during the rush. | **Informs and Enriches:** When a new session starts, a simple "hint" hydrates a high-speed cache with the user’s complete 360-degree profile, like pulling a client file before they walk into the office. |

The Three Layers of Cognitive Memory (Cold Path)

1. **Identity:** **Who the user is.** (Name, role, user ID). Stable, factual attributes.  
2. **Preferences:** **What the user wants.** (Project budgets, product constraints, specific rules of engagement).  
3. **Traits:** **How the user behaves.** (Technical expert vs. novice, communication style like formal/informal). Allows the AI to build genuine rapport.

Informing Your AI (Hot Path)

We pivot away from traditional AI gateways by *not* forcing you to route mission-critical traffic through our servers, avoiding bottlenecks.

1. **Quick Call:** Your application makes a quick, 20-millisecond call to our **Template API**.  
2. **Enrichment:** You send a prompt template with simple placeholders (e.g., `[[communication_style]]`). We instantly inject the user's pre-computed context.  
3. **Control:** The fully enriched, intelligent prompt is returned to you. Your application then sends it directly to the LLM of your choice (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.). You remain in complete control of your AI stack, security, and costs.
